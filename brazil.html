Brazil

New Time

  • Year: 2020
  • Typology: Mixed use
  • Status: Under construction
  • Role: Lead Architect
  • Softwares: Revit, Lumion and Photoshop

Located on the beginning of the most touristic beach of the city, the layout of this strategic mixed-use has been carefully considered to create a development of distinction, with a variety of residential units for rental purposes, high quality commercial spaces and exemplary building designs.

The scheme includes 15 floors with incorporation of alternating floors with apartments and living areas that fosters a unique relationship between functionality and the surrounding environment, offering a new perspective for occupants. By deconstructing the conventional notion of floor types, each floor possesses its distinct layout and identity, creating a harmonious blend of shared, private, and public spaces.

Uberaba House

  • Year: 2020
  • Typology: Residential
  • Role: Assistant Architect
  • Softwares: Revit, Lumion and 3dsmax

The context for this house is in a condominium of private homes.

The scheme employs a collection of cuboid shapes, which deliver the front façade and back, creating a green terrace and capturing natural light in the depth of the house. On the back façade, the cubes create the 3 main rooms of the house, which have a beautiful view of the pool on the ground floor.

Velar

  • Year: 2019
  • Typology: Residential /Commercial
  • Status: In progress
  • Role: Design Architec
  • Softwares: Revit and Lumion

The project stemmed from a fundamental concept of designing vertical houses that offer uninterrupted views of a captivating 15-meter seafront. Emphasizing a seamless integration with the natural landscape, the project ensures an immersive experience for the residents. However, what sets this project apart is its visionary approach that extends beyond the immediate site boundaries. Recognizing the potential of the expanding city area, the project ingeniously incorporates adjacent land to create a harmonious fusion of private dwellings and high-quality commercial services and public spaces
